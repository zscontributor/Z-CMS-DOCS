Z-CMS Admin is where you manage content, media, themes, plugins and site members.

## Sign in

In a local environment, open `http://localhost:3101` to access Admin. With the default sample data, sign in with `admin@z-cms.org` and the password `admin123`. If two-factor authentication is enabled, enter the current code from your authenticator app after the password.

## First steps

1. Review the site name, time zone and language under **Settings**.
2. Open **Content** and create your first entry.
3. Upload images in **Media**.
4. Review menus and the active theme under **Appearance**.
5. Invite team members and assign the minimum required role.

:::tip
Grant Administrator access only to people who need to configure the whole site.
:::
